SUNRISE PRIMARY SCHOOL LIBRARY

OUR COMMUNITY

Our library is more than just a space for books;
it's a community hub where connections are forged and knowledge is shared.
We believe in fostering a sense of belonging and collaboration among students, teachers, and parents.

Book Clubs and Discussions
Engage in lively discussions and exchange thoughts on our featured books.
Join our book clubs to delve deeper into the pages and share your perspectives.
It's an opportunity to connect, learn, and expand horizons together.

Volunteer Opportunities
We welcome and encourage parent and community involvement.
Whether it's assisting during events, recommending books, or sharing your expertise, your active participation enriches our library and the educational journey of our students.

Celebrating Reading Together
Throughout the year, we celebrate the joy of reading through various events and initiatives.
From author visits to themed reading weeks, these activities create a vibrant environment where the love for books and learning is cherished and celebrated.

Connect and Engage
Connect with us on social media, or drop by the library to learn more about how you can be a part of our community.
Together, let's make reading not just a solitary activity but a shared, enriching experience for everyone at Sunrise Primary School.
